How It Works

Discover how easy it is to connect with customer services using our app.

For Users

1. Search for the Organization

Simply search for the organization you need to connect with using our app's search feature.

2. Choose Your Communication Method

Select whether you want to send a message or initiate a video call with the customer service representative.

3. Send Your Inquiry

Compose your message or start the video call to communicate directly with the organization's customer service team.

For Organizations

1. Receive Customer Inquiries

Organizations will receive messages or video call requests from users seeking assistance or information.

2. Respond Promptly

Customer service representatives can respond promptly to user inquiries, providing assistance and support as needed.

3. Seamless Communication

Enjoy seamless communication with users, ensuring a smooth experience for both parties without the need for external platforms.
